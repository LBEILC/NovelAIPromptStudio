## Design Context

### Users

NovelAI creators who drag generated images into a focused workbench to recover, edit, translate, classify, and copy Prompt Tags, and optionally save images in a simple local gallery. Vibe data is recovered from image metadata for read-only copying or export, not edited locally. The product must remain equally usable on Windows and macOS.

### Brand Personality

Precise, calm, and studio-grade. The interface should feel like a focused creative workbench: capable and technical without becoming cryptic, decorative, or visually noisy.

### Aesthetic Direction

A refined Lobe UI Tag workbench and image gallery with slate neutrals, a restrained blue primary color, quiet surfaces, and image-first composition. Use the locally bundled Geist, Geist Mono, and HarmonyOS Sans SC families consistently across platforms. Preserve a compact professional layout, but never achieve density by making text microscopic. Avoid neon sci-fi styling, glassmorphism, generic dashboard cards, ornamental motion, and one-sided accent strips for persistent selection states.

### Design Principles

1. Readability before density: primary working text is comfortable at normal viewing distance; captions are never smaller than 0.6875rem (11px).
2. Preserve the artwork as the visual focus while keeping prompt controls easy to scan and operate.
3. Use a small semantic type scale with consistent roles, weights, line heights, and tabular numerals for parameters.
4. Respect platform conventions for menus, title bars, shortcuts, paths, dialogs, and secure storage.
5. Prefer quiet, explicit state feedback over animation or decorative effects.
6. Express persistent selection with a complete tinted surface or full border. Reserve directional indicators for transient drag-and-drop placement only.
